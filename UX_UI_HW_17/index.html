User Research:

I use design thinking (empathize, define, ideate, prototype, and test) to reserach, design, and develop products with both the consumer and the stakeholder in mind. I am always sure to listen to what users and stakeholders want out of a product, and what the true goal is.

User Empathy:

My approach to user empathy is not only hearing what a user is telling me, but seeing what they aren't telling me. Watching facial experessions and analyzing infelctions unlocks a new level to language and comprehension.
